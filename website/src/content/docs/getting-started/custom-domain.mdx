## Add the domain

1. Open your app's **Domains** tab
2. Click **Add domain**
3. Enter your domain (e.g., `app.example.com`)

## Configure DNS

Point your domain to your server's IP address:

| Type | Name | Value |
|------|------|-------|
| A | `app` | `YOUR_SERVER_IP` |

For a root domain (`example.com`), some DNS providers require an ALIAS or ANAME record.

## SSL certificate

Caddy automatically provisions a Let's Encrypt certificate. This happens within 60 seconds of the DNS record propagating.

Check the domain's **SSL status** badge — it should show "active" with a green lock.

## Troubleshooting

- **DNS not propagating**: Wait up to 48 hours. Check with `dig app.example.com`
- **SSL error**: Verify the domain resolves to your server IP. Check Caddy logs.
- **Mixed content**: Ensure your app doesn't hardcode `http://` URLs
