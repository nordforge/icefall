## 502 Bad Gateway

**Cause**: Caddy can't reach the app container.

**Checklist**:
1. Is the container running? Check app status in the dashboard
2. Is the port correct? Check the detected port in app overview
3. Restart the app and check if the route updates

## SSL certificate errors

**Symptom**: Browser shows security warning or `ERR_CERT_AUTHORITY_INVALID`.

**Fix**:
1. Verify DNS points to your server: `dig your-domain.com`
2. Wait for DNS propagation (up to 48 hours)
3. Check Caddy logs for ACME errors
4. Ensure ports 80 and 443 are open on your firewall

## Can't reach app from internet

**Checklist**:
1. Firewall: ensure ports 80, 443, and 8443 are open
2. DNS: verify A record points to your server's public IP
3. Caddy: check if the route exists (`icefall domains list`)
4. Container: verify it's running and healthy
